Encoder Step Calculation for my drive system design

1. Wheel Circumference
-----------------------
Wheel Diameter = 21 mm
Circumference = π × diameter
              ≈ 3.1416 × 21
              ≈ 65.97 mm

2. Effective Counts per Wheel Revolution
-----------------------------------------
Encoder PPR (Pulses Per Revolution) = 10
Quadrature Decoding Mode = 4x
→ Counts per motor revolution = 10 × 4 = 40

Gear Ratio = 6.1
→ Motor spins 6.1 times for one full wheel revolution
→ Counts per wheel revolution = 40 × 6.1 = 244

3. Millimeters per Encoder Step
-------------------------------
mm per step = Wheel Circumference / Counts per wheel revolution
            = 65.97 mm / 244
            ≈ 0.2704 mm/step (good enough)
